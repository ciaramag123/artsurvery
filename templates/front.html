Art and Museum Collections Survey

This survey is part of a research project investigating the relationship between museum collections in Ireland and their surrounding socioeconomic and geographic factors. Museums play a crucial role in preserving cultural heritage, but their collections often reflect historical, social, and economic influences from their respective regions.

The purpose of this study is to understand how museum collections align with the characteristics of their local communities. By analyzing art preferences, we aim to explore whether there are correlations between public taste and the type of art displayed in these institutions.

How to Participate:

  • Select your county from the dropdown menu below.
  • Rate each painting on a scale of 1 to 5, where:
    • 5: You strongly like it.
    • 1: You strongly dislike it.
